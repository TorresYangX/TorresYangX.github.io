I am a junior undergraduate of Computer Science and Technology, department of Computer Science and Engineering, [Southern University of Science and Technology](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/). My research interest includes Nature Language Processing(NLP), Large Language Models(LLM), Data Mining.

I'm very fortunate to be advised by [Prof.Xuan Song](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/zh/faculties/songxuan.html) during my undergraduate study. I worked as a research assistant at Peking University from Aug 2023 to Mar 2024, under the supervision of [Prof.Haoran Zhang](https://urban.pkusz.edu.cn/info/1013/3002.htm). My research focus was on reconstructing low-quality trajectories with transformer variational autoencoder. At present, I’m working at Tokyo University as a research intern, under the supervision of [Prof.Shibasaki Ryosuke](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/focus/ja/people/people001890.html). My focus is on LLMs. Specifically, generating synthetic human mobility trajectories with LLM-based approaches.

I am currently pursuing a **Ph.D.** in Computer Science and am enthusiastic about enhancing my scientific research ability and experience.
